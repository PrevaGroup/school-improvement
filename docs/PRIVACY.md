# Privacy policy

This app is a working proof of concept: an experiment to advance agentic support to
help people use education data for better learning and workforce outcomes.

The service is free, so we track your AI usage to manage costs.

We also study that same usage data to improve the working proof of concept, the way
leading AI providers study their own internal data tools (see
[Anthropic](https://claude.com/blog/how-anthropic-enables-self-service-data-analytics-with-claude)
and [OpenAI](https://openai.com/index/inside-our-in-house-data-agent/)).
